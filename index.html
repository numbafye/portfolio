About

I was first introduced to coding through a video game called 'Graal' where I would write HTML tags and CSS properties into my bio so it would look cooler than everyone else's. I learned it by googling how other players would do it but never tried to use those codes outside of the game. A few years later I practiced coding during the beginning of the pandemic when I found out that Harvard had free online software development classes. However, it wasn't until I enrolled into NJIT's software development program that I started taking coding seriously. The courses really pushed me to my limits and taught me a lot about myself as well as a multitude of languages, frameworks, databases and architectural designs of websites. I am currently learning how to become more skilled in react and SASS. Outside of frontend development I enjoy playing my instruments and producing music, trying new restaurants and skateboarding. I strive towards bettering my skills everyday so that I can make an impact in me and my family's lives. Hope you enjoyed my portfolio! Have a blessed day.
